TicTacToe Rivals

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Raza's Dev Studio ("we", "us", or "our") handles user information in the TicTacToe Rivals mobile application (the "App") for Android devices distributed via the Google Play Store.

1. Introduction

TicTacToe Rivals is designed as a premium board game. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting it. The App does not require any account creation or user registration for core gameplay.

2. Information Collection

The App is built to operate primarily offline on your device.

  • No Personal Data Collected by Us: We do not collect, capture, store, or transmit any personal information, such as your name, email address, phone number, physical address, contacts, or calendar.
  • No Account Creation: You play the game without creating any profile linked to an email or external identity.
  • No Device Sensors/Permissions Access: The App does not access your location, camera, microphone, SMS, storage files, or background tracking systems.
  • Local Player Nicknames: Any username or nickname you set in the onboarding screen is stored purely locally on your device in local database files (Hive storage) and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

3. Data Usage

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not use your personal data.

  • Game statistics, levels, coin balance, and rank points (RP) are processed and saved entirely on your device.
  • Any gameplay metrics (such as rounds won, difficulty selections, and unlock status) are used solely to determine local achievements and progression milestones within the local game client.

4. Data Sharing

Except for limited device and diagnostic information processed by our third-party advertising partner Google AdMob (as detailed in Section 6), we do not share, sell, trade, rent, or otherwise disclose any user data, personal or non-personal, to any third-party companies, advertising networks, or analytics providers.

5. Children's Privacy

TicTacToe Rivals is fully compliant with the Google Play Families Policy and the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • The App does not collect any personal data from any user, including children under the age of 13.
  • The game does not contain multiplayer chat, social network integrations, or location tracking.
  • Advertisements served in the App are configured to comply with the Google Play Families Policy and COPPA requirements, displaying age-appropriate content.

6. Advertising

TicTacToe Rivals contains advertisements to support its development and keep the game free to play.

  • Ad Provider: These advertisements are provided by Google AdMob and may also be served through Google's advertising partners.
  • Information Collected by AdMob: To serve advertisements, Google AdMob and its partners may collect, process, and use limited device-related, advertising, diagnostic, and usage-related information (such as your device's advertising ID, IP address, device model, language settings, and diagnostic logs) in accordance with their own privacy policies.
  • Control and Consent: You can control personalized advertising settings or reset your identifier through your Android device settings (e.g., Settings > Privacy > Ads).
  • Reference Policies:
    • For details on how Google manages user data, visit the Google Privacy & Terms Policy.
    • To understand how Google uses information from apps that integrate their services, see How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.
    • For information regarding Google AdMob data collection, see the Google AdMob Privacy Disclosures.

7. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the Google AdMob SDK to display advertisements as detailed in Section 6. Other than Google AdMob, there are no other third-party SDKs, tracking libraries, analytics platforms, or social logins integrated into the App. Specifically:

  • The App does not use Firebase, Google Analytics, or other telemetry tools.
  • The App does not include social login features (no Facebook Login, Google Sign-In, etc.).

8. Data Security

Since all game progress, statistics, coins, and settings are saved locally on your device in the App's secure private sandbox:

  • The security of your data relies on the physical security of your Android device and Android's sandboxed filesystem.
  • Tapping the "Reset Progress" option in the game settings menu will permanently delete all local game statistics, player profiles, coins, and unlocks from the device.

9. User Rights

Under various privacy regulations (including GDPR, CCPA, and COPPA), users have rights regarding their data. Since the App is entirely offline-first:

  • You have absolute control over your local data.
  • You can access your data at any time inside the Profile, Stats, and Achievements screens.
  • You can erase all data by clearing the App's storage cache in your Android System Settings or by using the double-confirmed "Reset Progress" button in the in-game Settings.

10. Changes To This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in legal requirements or app features. Any updates will be posted on this page and will take effect immediately upon publication. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
